Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Asset Management in Odoo?

Asset Management is a comprehensive Odoo module that helps businesses track, manage, and maintain their physical assets throughout their lifecycle. It includes features for asset tracking, depreciation calculation, maintenance scheduling, transfer management, and financial reporting.

What types of assets can I manage?

You can manage various types of assets including office equipment, vehicles, furniture, computers, buildings, and any other physical assets your business owns. The module supports both single assets and multiple stock-managed assets with quantity tracking.

How does depreciation work in this module?

The module supports multiple depreciation methods including percentage-based and fixed amount depreciation. You can set yearly, monthly, or daily depreciation frequencies, and the system automatically calculates depreciation based on your configured rules. Depreciation can be applied to either the original purchase price or the current book value.

Can I track asset assignments to employees?

Yes! The module includes comprehensive transfer management that tracks when assets are assigned to employees, who assigned them, assignment dates, return dates, and current status. Each transfer gets a unique transfer code for easy tracking and audit purposes.

How do I handle maintenance and repairs?

The maintenance module allows you to record maintenance activities, track costs, manage vendor relationships, and monitor repair status. You can attach documents, track maintenance history, and generate reports on maintenance expenses and vendor performance.

What reporting capabilities are available?

The module provides comprehensive reporting including asset value reports, depreciation analysis, maintenance cost reports, transfer history, stock movement analysis, and vendor performance reports. All reports can be filtered by date ranges, asset types, and other criteria.

Is there demo data available?

Yes! The module includes comprehensive demo data with sample asset types, vendors, tags, assets, transfers, maintenance records, and depreciation entries. This helps you understand the module's capabilities and provides examples to follow when setting up your own assets.

How do I get started with the module?

Start by configuring asset types with depreciation rules, then set up vendor information and asset tags. Create your first assets and explore the demo data to understand workflows. The module includes detailed documentation and step-by-step guides to help you get up and running quickly.
